Flavour

- Instant recoloration at its finest -


- How it works -

In Flavour, elements colors are attribute-base, meaning that when the attribute changes, the color does too

Tags

1.<a flavour=1>Colorful button</a>
2.<p flavour-text=1>Much color. Such Flavour. Wow</p>

Triggers

1.<a flavour-text=1 flavour=1 class="btn flavour-setup">Red</a>
2.<a flavour-text=3 flavour=3 class="btn flavour-setup">Blue</a>
3.<a flavour-text=4 flavour=4 class="btn flavour-setup">Green</a>
- Try it -
Useless button

Much color. Such Flavour. Wow


Click on of those buttons to change the whole page's flavour
